# Privacy Policy for Browse Tracker

**Last Updated:** January 4, 2026

## Overview

Browse Tracker is a browser extension designed to help you track and understand your browsing habits. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it's stored, and how it's used.

## Data Collection

Browse Tracker collects the following information:

### Website Activity
- Domain names of websites you visit
- Time spent on each website
- Number of visits to each website
- Timestamp of last visit

### YouTube Activity
- Names of YouTube channels you watch
- Duration of time spent watching each channel

### What We DON'T Collect
- Full URLs or page content
- Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- Passwords or login credentials
- Credit card or payment information
- Search queries or form data
- Your IP address
- Location data

## Data Storage

All data collected by Browse Tracker is stored **locally on your device** using the browser's local storage API (`browser.storage.local`). 

**Important:**
- No data is transmitted to any external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- No data leaves your device
- No analytics or tracking services are used
- No network requests are made by this extension

## Data Usage

The collected data is used solely to:
- Display statistics about your browsing habits in the extension popup
- Send hourly notifications summarizing your recent activity
- Help you understand how you spend time online

## Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

### View Your Data
Click the extension icon to view all collected statistics at any time.

### Delete Your Data
Click the "Clear All Data" button in the extension popup to permanently delete all tracked information.

### Disable Tracking
You can disable or uninstall the extension at any time through your browser's extension settings. All data will be removed when you uninstall.

## Permissions Explanation

Browse Tracker requests the following browser permissions:

- **`tabs`** - To track which websites you visit and how long you spend on them
- **`storage`** - To save your browsing statistics locally on your device
- **`notifications`** - To send hourly activity summaries
- **`alarms`** - To schedule hourly notifications
- **`<all_urls>`** - To track activity across all websites you visit

These permissions are used exclusively for the functionality described above.

## Third-Party Services

Browse Tracker does not integrate with, communicate with, or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Browse Tracker does not knowingly collect information from children. The extension is designed for general use and stores all data locally without any age verification or personal information collection.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data is as secure as your device and browser
- We recommend keeping your browser and operating system updated
- Use browser security features like master passwords if available
- Only you have access to your browsing statistics
